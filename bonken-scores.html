Bonken

Bonken is a collection of fun little card games for four persons, which are combined to form a final score. The calculation of the score of each mini-game and of the overall score, however, is not the simplest. This web-app makes life easier by calculating the score for you. It also keeps track of whose turn it is to shuffle, deal, pick a mini-game, and who is bidding against who.

For details on the rules of the game (and its score calculation), see somewhere on the internet.

Some notes about this web-app:

  • This page contains everything needed: you don't need to be online to calculate the scores.
  • Reloading the page, or using the (brower's) back button, will erase any scores stored on this page. You can, however, safely navigate on this page using the page's menu.
